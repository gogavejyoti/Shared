## Digital Resource Planning (Digital RP) Platform

### Key Business Capabilities

* **100% In-House Enterprise Platform** with an intuitive spreadsheet-like interface, enabling users to create customized resource plans without dependency on Excel.
* **Centralized Planning Portal** for creating, managing, and maintaining workforce plans in a secure, governed environment.
* **Automatic Recovery & Data Protection** to safeguard user work against unexpected system interruptions.
* **Version Management** allowing users to create multiple planning scenarios, clone existing plans, compare versions, and manage visibility with a single click.
* **Rapid Plan Creation** through standardized FTE and Transaction templates with pre-built business formulas and support for new Line of Business (LOB) onboarding.
* **Executive Staffing Dashboard** providing real-time visibility into key workforce metrics including FTE Required, FTE Available, Staffing Gap, Delta, and capacity insights.
* **Advanced Analytics & Visualization** featuring graphical trends for Volumes, Shrinkage, Attrition, and comparison across multiple Lines of Business.
* **Scenario Comparison** enabling side-by-side evaluation of different resource planning versions to support informed business decisions.
* **Assumption Simulator** for modeling workforce scenarios by adjusting key planning drivers such as Shrinkage, Attrition, and AHT to instantly forecast FTE requirements.
* **Machine Learning Forecasting** leveraging time-series models and historical trends to predict future planning assumptions and workforce demand.
* **AI-Powered Recommendations & Risk Alerts** that identify planning risks by comparing actual vs. planned metrics, provide proactive alerts, and recommend mitigation actions to improve planning accuracy.
